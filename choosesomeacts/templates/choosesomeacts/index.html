This is a prototype version of a very simple tool I am trying to build so I can easily...

  • see who is playing at a festival
  • pre-listen to some of their tracks if I don't know the act
  • mark them as "really want to see", "yeah, why not?" and "this causes pain in my ears.."

In the long run, I also would like to

  • create a personal running order from it
  • that can be auto-updated if the festival crew posts shifts in the overall running order
  • and informs me about potential overlapping conflicts where I can decide what to prioritize
  • oh and I'd love to match my running order with that of friends!

This really early test version only works for the Fusion 2013 festival with minimum features.
